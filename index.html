Ritu Raj Lamsal

Biography

Ritu Raj Lamsal obtained his Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics and Communication Engineering from the Institution of Engineers, India, in 1999 and completed his Master of Technology in VLSI Design from Bharat University, Chennai, in 2006, where he graduated as the top rank holder. During his doctoral studies at the University of Malaga, Spain, he submitted a thesis focused on IoT innovations for agricultural applications. With over two decades of teaching experience at the university level in various roles, he is well-versed in designing and developing IoT-based hardware-software co-design solutions. In recognition of his professional contributions, he was awarded Fellow Membership in the Institution of Engineers, India, in 2017.He is also a peer reveiwer for highly indexed and scopus journals like IEEE Acess, Springer Nature.
